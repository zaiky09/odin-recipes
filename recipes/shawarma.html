Shawarma

Description

Chicken shawarma is a flavorful Middle Eastern dish made from marinated chicken that is stacked on a vertical rotisserie. As the chicken slowly cooks and rotates, it is thinly shaved off and typically served in a pita or flatbread. The dish is often garnished with toppings like fresh vegetables, pickles, and sauces such as garlic sauce, tahini, or hummus. The marinade, usually a mix of spices, yogurt, and lemon juice, gives chicken shawarma its distinctive taste.

Ingredients

Marinade:

  • Chicken thighs or breasts, boneless and skinless
  • Yogurt
  • Olive oil
  • Lemon juice
  • Garlic, minced
  • Ground cumin
  • Ground coriander
  • Ground paprika
  • Ground turmeric
  • Ground cinnamon
  • Ground cloves
  • Ground black pepper
  • Salt

Serving:

  • Pita bread or flatbread
  • Fresh vegetables (lettuce, tomatoes, cucumbers, red onions)
  • Pickles
  • Fresh parsley
  • Tahini sauce or garlic sauce

Steps

  1. Combine Ingredients: In a bowl, mix together yogurt, olive oil, lemon juice, minced garlic, ground cumin, ground coriander, ground paprika, ground turmeric, ground cinnamon, ground cloves, ground black pepper, and salt.
  2. Marinate the Chicken: Add the boneless, skinless chicken thighs or breasts to the marinade, ensuring they are well-coated. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour, preferably overnight for the best flavor.
  3. Preheat Oven or Grill: Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C) or prepare a grill for medium-high heat.
  4. Oven Method: Place the marinated chicken on a baking sheet and bake for 25-30 minutes, or until fully cooked, turning halfway through.
  5. Rest and Slice: Allow the cooked chicken to rest for a few minutes, then slice it thinly.
  6. Chop Vegetables: Slice fresh vegetables like lettuce, tomatoes, cucumbers, and red onions.
  7. Warm the Pita: Heat pita bread or flatbread in the oven or on a skillet until warm and pliable.
  8. Add Chicken and Toppings: Place sliced chicken in the center of the pita. Add fresh vegetables, pickles, and parsley.
  9. Drizzle Sauces: Drizzle tahini sauce or garlic sauce over the top. Add hummus if desired.
  10. Wrap and Serve: Fold the pita around the fillings, wrap it up, and serve immediately.
